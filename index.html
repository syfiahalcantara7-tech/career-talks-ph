✨ Choosing a Course Without Panic

Feeling pressured about college choices? You're not alone. Many students struggle between passion, practicality, and family expectations.

Start by identifying your interests, strengths, and values. Remember: your course is a starting point, not your entire future.

📚 Student Survival Tips

Student life can be overwhelming. Balancing academics, friendships, and expectations is hard.

Take breaks, sleep properly, and avoid comparing your progress to others. Growth looks different for everyone.
